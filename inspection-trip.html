Travel Notes

Tips for the Trip

🚗

One-Way Car Rental

Book a one-way rental from CLT to ORF well in advance. One-way fees vary but the routing is worth it — no backtracking to Charlotte. Enterprise and Hertz both service both airports.

👶

19-Month-Old on the Road

No drive exceeds 2.5 hrs. Budget an extra 30–45 min per drive for a stop. Staying close to each plant means no early-morning wake-ups. Pack the white-noise machine and keep nap time sacred.

🏨

Where to Stay

Charlotte: airport corridor hotels are convenient. Henderson, NC: better options than Warrenton itself. Kinston/Goldsboro: solid mid-trip base. Virginia Beach oceanfront: Airbnbs ideal — beach access, kitchen, space for a toddler.

📋

Confirm Plant Hours

Lucas Concrete's hours weren't listed — call ahead at 704-525-9622. All plants are Mon–Fri only. Confirm your inspection schedule dates as soon as possible, as plants need advance notice for APA visits.

⚠️

Seatack Is the Wild Card

The 4 PM close at Seatack is the tightest constraint on the whole trip. Do not push this to Friday. A 8 AM Thursday start gives you a 4-hour buffer. If the drive from LaGrange runs long, call ahead.

🏖

Virginia Beach Finish

This is a genuinely great spot to end the trip. Virginia Aquarium & Marine Science Center is excellent with toddlers. ORF is only 20 min away, small and easy. Consider flying home Saturday for a full beach day Friday.
